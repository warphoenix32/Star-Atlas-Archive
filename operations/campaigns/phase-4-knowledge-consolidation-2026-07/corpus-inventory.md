## Editorial interpretation

The repository does not need 81 public pages. Many current files are registries,
source profiles, research backlogs, or domain indexes that serve curators and
researchers. Phase 4 should consolidate them into a smaller number of coherent
historical dossiers while preserving the underlying knowledge pages for
traceability.

Compact indexes are not automatically weak pages. The principal depth gap is
lore: the Archive contains a large canonical taxonomy snapshot, but Knowledge
currently offers only a domain guide and canon registry rather than
reader-oriented histories of the setting, factions, species, locations, and
narratives.
