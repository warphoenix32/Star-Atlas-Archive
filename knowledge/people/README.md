# People in Star Atlas History

This domain documents people and public identities whose work materially shaped Star Atlas products, institutions, governance, media, guilds, and community history. Roles are dated; aliases are evidence-based; inclusion is not endorsement.

- [Major Actor Index](Actor-Master-Index.md) — the discovery registry, attribution rules, and priority research queue.
- [Krigs Source Profile](Krigs-Source-Profile.md) — HNN founder/operator and community-journalism source handling.

## Reading person-level evidence

Unknown-speaker transcripts can preserve important information when the recording and timestamp are exact, but they cannot support a named quotation or authority claim without separate attribution. Discord tags and historical titles similarly require context and dates.
