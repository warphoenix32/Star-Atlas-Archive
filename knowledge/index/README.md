# Repository Indexes

Indexes help researchers find the right entity or source without turning a catalog entry into a factual conclusion. Entity registration, source discovery, archive completeness, and claim authority remain separate questions.

## Identity and source indexes

- [Entity Registry](Entity-Registry.md) — stable institutional, product, asset, token, lore, and community identities.
- [Public Source Registry](source-registry/Public-Source-Registry.md) — official and community source families, authority limits, and collection states.
- [Audited Community Source Profiles](source-registry/Audited-Community-Source-Profiles.md) — approved archival use for Aephia, Intergalactic Herald, HNN, The Hologram, and Krigs.

## Official publication indexes

- [Official Newsroom Index](source-registry/Official-Newsroom-Index.md) — chronological official publication inventory with known coverage limits.
- [Official Newsroom Extraction Batch 001](source-registry/Official-Newsroom-Extraction-Batch-001.md) — three early high-value article extractions and unresolved follow-up work.

## Discovery snapshots

- [Community Content URL Inventory, 2026-07-12](source-registry/Community-Content-URL-Inventory-2026-07-12.md) — a historical Discord-derived URL discovery snapshot, not an authorship or completeness ledger.
